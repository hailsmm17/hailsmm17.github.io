How to make rice

Typically, rice is 2 parts water to 1 part rice: 2:1

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add 1 cup of rice to boiling water
  3. Cook for:
    1. 20 mins for white rice/li>
    2. 30 mins for brown rice
